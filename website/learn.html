VISIT BONWIRE

Bonwire is a small town in the Ashanti Region of Ghana, globally renowned as the home of authentic Kente weaving. For centuries, it has preserved the rich tradition of handwoven Kente, a cloth deeply rooted in royalty, culture, and identity. Step into the vibrant village of Bonwire, where every thread tells a story and every pattern holds centuries of wisdom. As the birthplace of authentic Kente cloth, Bonwire invites you to explore the rich traditions, meet skilled weavers, and experience the beauty of Ghanaian culture firsthand. Watch. Weave. Wear.
